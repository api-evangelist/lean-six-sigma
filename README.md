# Lean Six Sigma (lean-six-sigma)
Lean Six Sigma is a methodology that combines Lean manufacturing principles and Six Sigma quality management techniques to improve business processes by eliminating waste and reducing variation, focusing on data-driven decision making and continuous improvement.
